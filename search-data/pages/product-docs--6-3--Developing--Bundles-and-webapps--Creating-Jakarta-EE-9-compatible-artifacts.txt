# Creating Jakarta EE 9 compatible artifacts

The setups available in the following repositories use the [Eclipse Transformer](https://github.com/eclipse/transformer) to transform the binaries of the existing artifacts. This allows you to create Jakarta EE 9 compatible artifacts deployable on Tomcat 10:

-   Magnolia CE: [bitbucket.org/magnolia-cms/ce-ee9/](https://bitbucket.org/magnolia-cms/ce-ee9/)

-   Magnolia DX Core: [git.magnolia-cms.com/projects/PLATFORM/repos/dx-core-ee9/](https://git.magnolia-cms.com/projects/PLATFORM/repos/dx-core-ee9/) (restricted access)
