# Magnolia Publications deep dive

## [](#_sidebar_filters)Sidebar Filters

The sidebar filters allow you to refine your view by selecting specific criteria.

-   **Release**: Select the namespace to analyze. Defaults to the first available option.

-   **Date range**: Select the time period using preset ranges or the calendar picker for custom dates.

-   **Memory unit**: Choose how to display data transfer sizes (KB, MB, GB, or TB).


Click **Apply filters** to update the view.

## [](#_request)Request

Monitor publication activity and success rates.

-   **Number of publications**: Total publication count and trends over time.

-   **Number of unpublications**: Unpublication count, ratio, and trends over time.

-   **Success ratio**: Percentage of successful operations and trends over time.

-   **Top 10 error messages**: Most common errors affecting your publishing workflow.


## [](#_response_time)Response time

Track how quickly content reaches your Public instances.

-   **Latency**: Average response time for publication operations.

-   **Latency 95th percentile**: Response time threshold that 95% of operations fall within.

-   **Latency per subscriber**: Response times broken down by each subscriber instance.


## [](#_transactions)Transactions

Monitor transaction health for publication commits.

-   **Rollback ratio**: Percentage of transactions that failed and were rolled back.


## [](#_traffic)Traffic

Understand bandwidth consumption from publishing operations.

-   **Traffic**: Total data transferred and trends over time.


## [](#_workspaces)Workspaces

Analyze publication activity across your JCR workspaces.

-   **Total per workspace**: Publication counts for each workspace.

-   **Total failures per workspace**: Failed publication attempts for each workspace.


## [](#_publishers)Publishers

Identify content authoring patterns across your team.

-   **Top 10 publishers**: Most active users publishing content.
