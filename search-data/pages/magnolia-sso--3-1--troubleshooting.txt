# Troubleshooting

This page contains troubleshooting tips for the Magnolia SSO module.

Unresolved include directive in modules/ROOT/pages/troubleshooting.adoc - include::6.2@product-docs:ROOT:partial$i/\_Known-issues.adoc\[\]

## [](#_turn_on_debug_logs)Turn on `DEBUG` logs

When facing authentication or configuration issues with the SSO module, it may be helpful to turn on your `DEBUG` logs. [Pac4j](https://www.pac4j.org) logs in particular are highly informative regarding request handling, redirects, and validating credentials.

### [](#_instructions)Instructions

1.  Add the following [loggers](#6.2@product-docs:ROOT:/Administration/Monitoring.adoc#_loggers) to your `log4j2.xml` configuration file:

    log4j2.xml

    ```xml
    <Logger name="org.pac4j" level="DEBUG"/>
    <Logger name="info.magnolia.sso" level="DEBUG"/>
    ```
