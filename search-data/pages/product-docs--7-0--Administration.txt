# Administration

Digital experience administrators typically make high level decisions related to setup, user management, configuration, technical requirements, language needs, how metrics are used, and oversee vital security operations. They often act as a guide to developers and authors as well as monitor the overall health and user experience of a digital experience.

This section within our DX Core documentation guides you on accomplishing these very things. See the subpages below or in the navigation to get started.
