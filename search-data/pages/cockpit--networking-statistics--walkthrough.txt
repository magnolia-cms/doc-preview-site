# Networking statistics deep dive

## [](#_sidebar_filters)Sidebar Filters

The sidebar filters allow you to refine your view by selecting specific criteria.

-   **Release**: Select the release such as `prod`.

-   **Date Range**: Select the time period using preset ranges or the calendar picker for custom dates.

-   **Memory Unit**: Choose how to display data volumes (KB, MB, GB, or TB).

-   **Hosts**: Filter by specific hostnames or domains (supports multiple selections).


Click **Apply filters** to update the view.

## [](#_availability)Availability

View overall ingress availability. The average availability summarizes the period, while the time series highlights changes over time.

## [](#_requests)Requests

See total and per-interval request volumes. Use the protocol breakdown to understand HTTP/HTTPS usage patterns.

## [](#_round_trip)Round trip

Analyze round-trip time (RTT), including averages and distribution, to identify network latency trends across your ingress path.

## [](#_duration)Duration

Review response-time duration to spot slow responses and potential backend or network bottlenecks.

## [](#_traffic)Traffic

Monitor overall and per-interval traffic volumes and rates to understand throughput patterns and peaks.

## [](#_http_statuses)HTTP statuses

Examine status code distributions, error ratios, and 3xx/4xx/5xx trends to diagnose client or server issues.

## [](#_redirect)Redirect

View redirect ratios to understand how often requests result in redirects. The time series shows redirect behavior patterns across the selected period.
