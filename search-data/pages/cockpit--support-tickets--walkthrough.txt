# Support tickets deep dive

## [](#_status)Status

You can view an overview of support tickets related to your DX Cloud project. Select one or more of the following status filters to narrow down the list of tickets:

-   To do

-   In progress

-   Done


ðŸ”Ž Additionally, you can search for specific tickets using the search bar.

## [](#_id)ID

The ID is a unique identifier for each support ticket. This is typically HELPDESK- followed by a number, such as `HELPDESK-1234`.

## [](#_title)Title

The title information pulled from the support ticket.

## [](#_status_category)Status category

The larger status category of the support ticket. This includes:

-   To do

-   In progress

-   Done


## [](#_status_2)Status

The current status of the support ticket.

## [](#_reporter)Reporter

The person who reported the support ticket.

## [](#_type)Type

The type of support ticket.

## [](#_created)Created

The date and time when the support ticket was created.

-   **Format**: MONTH DAY, YEAR, TIME

-   **Example**: Aug 1, 2026, 12:42 PM
