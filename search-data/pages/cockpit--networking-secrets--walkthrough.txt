# Secrets deep dive

## [](#_environment)Environment

Choose your **Enviroment** in the dropdown. This is typically `prod` or `dev`.

## [](#_type)Type

Filter by secret type:

-   Opaque

-   Registry

-   Basic Auth


## [](#_add)Add

Add a secret.

1.  Go to your Cockpit and navigate to Networking > Secrets.

2.  Choose the Environment.

3.  Click Add.

4.  Give the secret a name.

5.  Choose the secret type.

    -   **Opaque**:

        -   Enter the key and value.


    -   **Registry**:

        -   Enter the Domain name. (e.g, `registry.magnolia-platform.com`)

        -   Enter the User.

        -   Enter the Password.


    -   **Basic Auth**:

        -   Enter the user.

        -   Enter the password.




## [](#_name)Name

The name of the secret. You specified this name when adding the secret in the Cockpit.

## [](#_type_2)Type

The type of secret. This is either Opaque, Registry, or Basic Auth.

## [](#_data)Data

The secret data. This depends on the secret type.

-   **Opaque**: includes key-value pairs (as environment variables).

-   **Registry**: includes the domain.

-   **Basic Auth**: specifies the data is user and password.


## [](#_created)Created

The date when the secret was created.

-   **Format**: Month, DD, YYYY, HH:MM AM/PM

-   **Example**: March 17, 2026, 4:20 PM


## [](#_updated)Updated

The date when the secret was last updated.

-   **Format**: Month, DD, YYYY, HH:MM AM/PM

-   **Example**: March 17, 2026, 4:20 PM
