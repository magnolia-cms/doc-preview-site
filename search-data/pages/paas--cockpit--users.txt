# Managing your user profile

To manage your user profile from the Cockpit, select the user icon in the top right corner.

## [](#_accessing_dx_cloud)Accessing DX Cloud

When you sign up to DX Cloud, your credentials grant you access via SSO so that you only need to login once, creating a streamlined user experience whether you are a developer, author, or administrator.

### [](#_log_in_to_dx_cloud)Log in to DX Cloud:

1.  Go to your dedicated DX Cloud URL.

    This is typically `https://cockpit.YOUR_COMPANY.magnolia-platform.com/`. You receive the URL during your onboarding process. If you need assistance, contact [DX Cloud support](https://magnolia-cms.atlassian.net/servicedesk/customer/portal/13).

2.  Enter your credentials.

3.  Start creating amazing content with DX Cloud.

    ![login to paas](../_images/login-to-paas.png)


### [](#_log_out_of_dx_cloud)Log out of DX Cloud

1.  In the top right corner, click the logout icon.


| Ref | Preference | Description |
| --- | --- | --- |
| **1** | `Language` | Select your user language from the drop-down menu. Currently supported languages • English • Deutsch • Español • Schwizerdütsch • Polski • Français |
| **2** | `Theme` | Choose your UI theme for the Cockpit. • **Dark** = high contrast black background with bright color graphs, etc. • **Light** = light background with a standard Magnolia palette • **Auto** = detects your browser settings |
| **3** | `Email notifications` | Toggle email notifications on or off. When `on` , you’ll receive email alerts on the health and status of your deployment. This includes any relevant [Manage DX Cloud](../) . |

| Icon | Level | Description |
| --- | --- | --- |
| ** | **Info** | Info notifications inform you of minor updates and/or items that do not require your attention. |
| ** | **Warning** | Warning notifications inform you of events that might require your attention. Examples • release • bug fixes • maintenance windows |
| ** | **Critical** | Critical notifications inform you of events that require your attention. Examples • an instance is down • upcoming changes that may temporarily limit development |
