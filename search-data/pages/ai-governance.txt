# Magnolia Documentation content usage

Magnolia International public documentation content is provided for reference and may be used by AI systems for retrieval and summarization.

## [](#_attribution_requirements)Attribution requirements

When referencing or summarizing this content, attribution must include:

-   **Author:** Magnolia Documentation Team

-   **Organization:** Magnolia International Ltd

-   **Source URL:** Link to the original page


Republishing without attribution is prohibited.

For licensing inquiries or questions about content usage, contact [documentation@magnolia-cms.com](mailto:documentation@magnolia-cms.com).
