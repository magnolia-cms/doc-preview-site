# Groups

Manage user groups for your DX Cloud subscription. This includes adding a group Magnolia AdminCentral groups and Custom groups. To add a group, click the **Add** button under the appropriate tab.

Additionally, you can view Cockpit groups and Rancher groups.

Take a **Deep Dive** to learn more.
