# Glossary

Action

An operation performed by Magnolia in response to a command, such as saving a page or deleting an asset. Certain actions triggered by Smart Chat require your explicit approval.

AdminCentral

The primary web-based management interface for Magnolia where authors create and manage content.

AI Accelerator

A suite of native AI capabilities that extends Magnolia’s functionality, enabling features like automated SEO metadata generation and Smart Chat itself.

AI-generated content

Any text, images, or other media created by artificial intelligence based on user prompts or instructions.

Approval workflow

A security mechanism where Smart Chat pauses to request user permission before executing critical actions, ensuring the user remains in control.

Component

A reusable content building block in Magnolia pages that can contain text, images, or other elements.

Content app

A specific tool within Magnolia (such as Pages, Assets, or Stories) designed for managing a particular type of data.

Content variant

A version of content personalized for specific audience segments, used in targeted marketing campaigns.

Context awareness

The system’s ability to respond based on the user’s current environment, including the active app and selected content items.

Fine-tuning

Customizing an AI model for specific tasks or to match your brand’s voice and style.

GEO (Generative Engine Optimization)

The process of optimizing content for AI-powered search engines (like Google SGE, Bing Chat, Perplexity) and voice assistants through structured data and AI-optimized content.

Hyper Prompt

A specialized prompt engineering framework that helps refine instructions to the LLM for more accurate, brand-aligned results.

Large Language Model (LLM)

The AI "brain" trained on massive datasets that allows Smart Chat to understand and generate human-like text.

Metadata

Data about content (titles, descriptions, tags) that helps organize, find, and manage your digital assets.

Multi-turn

An interaction style where Smart Chat remembers previous messages in a conversation, allowing for follow-up questions.

Multimodal AI

AI that can understand and work with different types of content (text, images, audio) in the same conversation.

Natural language

Conversational human language used to give instructions (such as "Show me recent uploads") rather than technical code.

Prompt

The natural language instruction or question you type into Smart Chat chat input.

Prompt engineering

The practice of crafting effective instructions for AI systems to get the best possible results.

Retrieval-augmented generation (RAG)

An AI technique that allows Smart Chat to pull relevant information from your specific Magnolia content to improve response accuracy.

SEO metadata

Specific metadata designed to improve search engine rankings, including page titles, descriptions, and keywords.

Session

The period of time you are active in the browser. Refreshing or closing the tab ends the session and resets Smart Chat’s conversation history.

Streaming response

Real-time text generation as the AI thinks and responds, allowing you to see answers appear as they’re being created.

Subapp

A functional tab within a Magnolia app, such as the "Browser" view for list navigation or the "Detail" view for editing.

Template

A predefined structure for creating consistent content, ensuring brand uniformity across pages and components.

Token

A piece of text that AI processes (roughly a word or part of a word) that affects how much content the AI can handle at once.

Tool

A specific function Smart Chat triggers to perform a task, such as "Generate Image," "Navigate to App," or "List Pages."
