# Reports help

Reports provide comprehensive summaries of your Magnolia DX Cloud subscription performance and resource usage over specific time periods.

These downloadable PDF reports compile all subscription dashboard metrics for compliance, analysis, planning, and stakeholder reporting.

-   **Weekly Reports**: 7-day summaries of all subscription metrics

-   **Monthly Reports**: 30-day comprehensive performance and usage analysis
