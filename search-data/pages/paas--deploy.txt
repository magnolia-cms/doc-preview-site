# Prerequisites

## [](#_deploy_dx_cloud)Deploy DX Cloud

To deploy DX Cloud, you need to make sure you meet the following prerequisites:

-   You must have [`kubectl`](https://kubernetes.io/docs/tasks/tools/#kubectl) installed.

-   You should be familiar with at least basic [`kubectl` commands](https://kubernetes.io/docs/reference/kubectl/kubectl/).

-   You must have [Helm](https://helm.sh/docs/intro/install/) installed.

-   You must have been provided [Rancher](https://rancher.com/) credentials by a member of the DX Cloud team.
