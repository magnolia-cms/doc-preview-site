# CDN statistics

Use this page to monitor CDN performance and troubleshoot issues at a glance.

-   **Availability**: Overall uptime and stability trends.

-   **Cache**: Hit ratio, coverage, and cache status distribution.

-   **Origin**: Zone-level traffic, latency, and response patterns.

-   **Requests**: Total volume, rates, and protocol mix.

-   **Round trip**: Network latency (RTT) over time.

-   **Duration**: Response time and per cache-status breakdowns.

-   **Traffic**: Throughput and volume trends.

-   **HTTP statuses**: Error ratios and 3xx/4xx/5xx distribution.


Each card has a **What is this?** section for metric details.
