# Create VWO account

You must have a VWO account in order to use the VWO AB Testing module. The instructions on this page guide you through setting up a VWO account.

> **Important:** The instructions and visuals on this page are current as of writing. However, for the most up-to-date details, see the offical VWO site.

## [](#_get_account_id)Get Account ID

1.  Go to [VWO](https://www.vwo.com/) and sign up for an account if you do not have one.

2.  Once you have verified your email and successful established a VWO account, go to the [VWO dashboard](https://app.vwo.com/#/dashboard).

3.  Go to **Settings** , and copy your Account ID.

    ![vwo account id](../_images/vwo_account_id.jpg)


## [](#_generate_api_token)Generate API token

1.  Go to your [VWO Developer Dashboard](https://app.vwo.com/#/developers).

2.  Go to the **Tokens** tab.

3.  Click **Add another API token**.

4.  Give your token a descriptive name. The `browse` permission is enough as Magnolia does not need to manipulate the data.

    > **Important:** The token is only displayed once. Keep it in a secure place. If you lose it, you have to generate a new token.

    ![vwo api key](../_images/vwo_api_key.jpg)


## [](#_generate_the_smartcode)Generate the SmartCode

1.  Go to your [VWO dashboard](https://app.vwo.com/#/dashboard).

2.  Go to **Configurations > Websites and Apps**.

3.  Click **Connect Now**.

    1.  Choose **Website**.

    2.  Enter your Magnolia public domain name.

    3.  Click **Add domain**.

        ![vwo add new site](../_images/vwo_add_new_site.jpg)


4.  VWO verifies the domain and generates the SmartCode.

    > **Tip:** You can wse the VWO Configuration Page to verify the connection between Magnolia and VWO once you have inserted the SmartCode into the <head> of your site.

    ![vwo smartcode](../_images/vwo_smartcode.png)
