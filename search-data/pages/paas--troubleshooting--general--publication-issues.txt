# Publication isn’t working

## [](#_symptom)Symptom

Publication doesn’t work in the environments.

## [](#_observations)Observations

First, you need to check if the receivers are configured correctly.

![publication](../../../_images/troubleshooting/publication.png)

> **Note:** If they are correct you need to check the superuser password.

## [](#_next_steps)Next steps

1.  Re-enable `superuser`.

2.  Synchronise the passwords in your `values.yaml` file.
