# Federations help

Federations enable your organization to integrate external Identity Providers (IdPs) with our DX Cloud platform through OpenID Connect (OIDC) protocols or SAML. This interface allows administrators to establish trusted relationships with third-party identity systems, configure Cockpit and Rancher mappings, as well as define user attribute mappings between your external IdP and platform resources.

Take a **Deep Dive** to learn more.
