# Operations

![useCase operations](../../_images/features/useCase-operations.jpg)

Magnolia excels in providing tools for cache management and operations, enhancing the performance and efficiency of digital experiences. The platform offers sophisticated caching mechanisms that optimize content delivery, ensuring faster page load times and improved responsiveness. Our cache tools allow administrators to configure and manage caching strategies, minimizing server load and enhancing scalability.

Additionally, we provide comprehensive operational features, enabling seamless maintenance tasks, updates, and monitoring. With intuitive operations tools, Magnolia empowers administrators to efficiently handle various aspects of the content management system, ensuring a smooth and reliable digital environment for users.

## [](#_operations_modules)Operations modules

Magnolia modules are shipped to you in different ways depending on your license:

-   **Community Edition (CE)**: Modules are included as part of the free and open-source version.

-   **DX Core**: Modules are included as part of the DX Core enterprise product. These modules are in addition to those in the Community Edition license where bundled.

-   **Special**: Some modules need their own special license as they are not included with CE or DX Core licenses. In some cases, an additional license is required.

    > **Tip:** For more details, see License.
