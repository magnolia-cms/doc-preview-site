# Health Deployments

Health Deployments monitoring provides real-time insights into your Magnolia DX Cloud environmentâ€™s deployments. Here you can see:

-   General **Deployments Health Score**

-   The total number of **Healthy checks**

-   The total number of **Warning checks**
