# Personas app

The Personas app allows you to create hypothetical visitors who represent the target audience. Use personas together with segmentation in the [Preview app](../Preview-app/) to test content variants.

See [Personalization: Personas](../../../use-cases/Personalization/) for more information on how to use the app.

> **Note:** The Personas app is available only in DX Core.

## [](#_configuration)Configuration

The Personas app is installed by the [Personalization module](../../../Modules/List-of-modules/Personalization-module/). The app is configured in `/personalization-personas-app/apps/personas-app.yaml`.

### [](#_workspace)Workspace

The Personas app operates on the `personas` workspace. The `personas` workspace stores the persona content.

### [](#_node_types)Node types

The Personas app operates on the custom `mgnl:persona` [node type](../../../Administration/Architecture/Node-types/) and on folders.
