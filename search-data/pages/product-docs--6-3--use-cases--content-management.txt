# Content management

![useCase contentManagement](../../_images/features/useCase-contentManagement.jpg)

Magnolia is a digital experience platform that empowers companies in efficiently handling their website content. With a user-friendly interface and intuitive tools, we make it easy to create, edit, and publish diverse content types, fostering a seamless content management process.

The modular architecture supports easy integration with various third-party applications and systems, enhancing overall digital experience. Our scalability and open-source nature make it a versatile solution suitable for businesses of all sizes, providing a reliable foundation for effective content management and dynamic website experiences.

From importing and managing campaigns to translating and tagging content, Magnoliaâ€™s content management modules offer powerful solutions to real-world problems.

## [](#_content_management_modules)Content management modules

Magnolia modules are shipped to you in different ways depending on your license:

-   **Community Edition (CE)**: Modules are included as part of the free and open-source version.

-   **DX Core**: Modules are included as part of the DX Core enterprise product. These modules are in addition to those in the Community Edition license where bundled.

-   **Special**: Some modules need their own special license as they are not included with CE or DX Core licenses. In some cases, an additional license is required.

    > **Tip:** For more details, see License.
