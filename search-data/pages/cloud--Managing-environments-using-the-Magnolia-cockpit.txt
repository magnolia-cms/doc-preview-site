# Managing environments using the Magnolia cockpit

In this section and its subpages, you learn how to manage environments.

An environment consists of one or several *[spaces](../Cockpit/Magnolia-cockpit-concepts/#_space)*, which group together related functionality needed to build or deliver a site. The systems that a space actually contains and how they are connected is defined by the *[spaceâ€™s setup](../Cockpit/Magnolia-cockpit-concepts/#_space_setup)*. A space is a container for one or more instances of Magnolia.

![image](../_images/gloss-env.png)

A cloud-based Magnolia package typically consists of three [environments](../Cockpit/Magnolia-cockpit-concepts/#_environment):

-   **Live**: Where the tested and approved version of the content product is delivered to customers.

-   **UAT** (User Acceptance Testing): Where the next version of the product is made available to editors and managers for testing and review.

-   **Integration**: Where developers try out ideas and test solutions before giving internal users access


## [](#_cloud_environments_vary)Cloud environments vary

Depending on your subscription package, the environments available to you may differ.

**For example:**

You may have only a single Live environment available, or only a UAT and a Live environment. In these cases, you can [deploy a development snapshot](../managing-updates-using-the-Magnolia-cockpit/deploying-snapshot/) directly in the Live or in the UAT environment respectively.

Environments are managed in the **Manage environments** page.

To access this page, in the **Package overview** page, click **Manage environments**.

![image](../_images/Envs-Change-envs-BAR_.png)

From the **Manage environments** page you can:

-   [Manage your log levels](Monitoring-and-logging/Managing-cloud-log-levels/)

-   [Restart an instance](Restarting-instances/)

-   [View RDS information](Viewing-cloud-database-information/)

-   [Set up a new environment](Setting-up-a-new-environment/)

-   [Tear down an environment](Tearing-down-an-environment/)

-   [Configure domain redirect rules](Configuring-domain-redirect-rules/)

-   [Protect environments from being torn down](Protecting-environments/)

-   [Back up and restore](Backing-up-and-restoring/)


![image](../_images/cloud-manage-environments-all-available.png)

> **Note:** The options available in Manage environments depend on the current state of your setup:If the Live environment is not set up, some actions are disabled.Backup related actions are only available for the Live environment.Unavailable actions are greyed out.
